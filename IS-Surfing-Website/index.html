Best Surf Spots!!!

New Port Beach

New Port Beach is home to "The Wedge" and iconic surf shops and restaurants. This is a great spot because it is easy to access and the waves are generaly calm.

The best Surf shops in the area are ranked below:

  1. Jack's Surf Shop
    • There are two locations in Newport
    • Often have the best deals
  2. Pj's Surf Shop
  3. Bilabong Surf shop
    • Billabong Website

Sunset Beach

Sunset Beach is located on the north shore of Oahu in Hawaii. These waves are often bigger here and not recomended for those with out experience.

Intresting facts about this beach:

  • Over two miles of nice sand
  • Home to various surfing competitions during the winter
  • Really clear water with lots of snorkling spots

Best times to surf

A general rule of thumb is that california has better surfing during the spring and summer months while hawaii has much better waves during the fall and winter. The swells and changes in seasons often contribute to this.
